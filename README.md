A modern **catalog + inventory + order capture** web application for small and growing businesses.  
It helps businesses **showcase products to customers**, manage stock, take orders during visits, and track basic product insights.

## Overview

**Distribution Catalog** is built to solve common problems faced by small distributors and retail businesses:

- Maintaining a digital product catalog
- Managing inventory/stock
- Capturing customer orders quickly (especially during visits)
- Saving orders for later packing & delivery
- Viewing product-level statistics and quick insights
